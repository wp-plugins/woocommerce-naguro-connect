== Description ==

This plugin connects your WooCommerce install to the Naguro platform.

[vimeo http://vimeo.com/118567722]

Increase your sales with smart online product configurators.

Help yourself and your clients with [Naguro](https://naguro.com/). Our tool enables your clients to customize full color printed products on your website. Try it for free. It won't be long before you wonder how you ever worked without it.

More sales, less effort. Just the way you like it!

You can find more information about [our service and API](https://naguro.com/) on our website.
